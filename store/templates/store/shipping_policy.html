7. Failed Delivery Attempts

If delivery fails due to:

  • Incorrect Address: Please verify your address during checkout
  • Customer Unavailable: Multiple delivery attempts will be made
  • Refused Delivery: Return shipping charges may apply
Important: Please ensure someone is available to receive the order. Courier partners typically attempt delivery 2-3 times before returning the package.

8. Damaged or Lost Shipments

  • Damaged Products: Report within 24 hours of delivery with photos
  • Lost Shipments: Contact us if order is not received within estimated time
  • Resolution: We will arrange for replacement or refund

Please inspect your order upon delivery and report any issues immediately.

Currently, we only ship within India. International shipping will be available soon. Stay tuned for updates!

10. Delivery Address Changes

Address changes can only be made before the order is dispatched. Once shipped, the delivery address cannot be modified. Please contact us immediately if you need to change your delivery address.
