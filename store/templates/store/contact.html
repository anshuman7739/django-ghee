Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number on our website to track your order or check the delivery status directly with our shipping partner.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery if the product is unopened and in its original packaging. Please contact our customer support team to initiate a return.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within India. We're working on expanding our shipping network to international locations soon.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping usually takes 3-5 business days for metro cities and 5-7 business days for other locations. Express shipping options are available at checkout.
