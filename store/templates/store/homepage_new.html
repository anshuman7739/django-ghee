Our Heritage

The Traditional Process

Our ghee is prepared using the traditional 'Bilona' method that has been passed down for generations. This method preserves all the natural nutrients and enhances the aroma.

1

Sourcing A2 Milk

We collect fresh milk from indigenous cows that produce only A2 protein milk, ensuring purity and quality.

2

Churning Curd

The milk is fermented into curd and then hand-churned to extract fresh butter using traditional equipment.

3

Slow Cooking

The butter is slowly simmered in copper vessels over low heat until all moisture evaporates, leaving behind pure ghee.
