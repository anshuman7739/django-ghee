About Us

Welcome to Gaumaatri — where purity meets tradition.

Our Story

We take pride in crafting authentic A2 handmade ghee the way it was meant to be — slowly, lovingly, and without a single machine involved. Every drop of our ghee is made in-house, following the ancient Bilona method, which preserves the essence, aroma, and nutritional richness of pure A2 milk.

Our process begins with milk from indigenous A2 cow breeds, cherished in Indian tradition for their wholesome and sattvic properties. The curd is hand-churned using wooden bilonas, and the butter is gently simmered over a slow flame until it releases its golden magic — pure, aromatic, and full of life.

At Gaumaatri, ghee is not just food; it's a sacred offering — a symbol of wellness, heritage, and divine nourishment. We believe that true luxury lies in simplicity, purity, and authenticity. That's why every jar of our ghee carries the warmth of a home and the blessings of tradition.

Bring home Gaumaatri A2 Handmade Ghee — crafted with care, rooted in tradition, and made to enrich your body, mind, and soul.

The Bilona Tradition

The ancient Bilona method is at the heart of what we do. This time-honored process involves:

  • Hand-churning cultured A2 curd using traditional wooden bilonas
  • Slow simmering of fresh butter over a gentle flame
  • Preserving all the natural nutrients, aroma, and golden goodness
  • Zero machines, zero shortcuts — just pure craftsmanship

Each batch is made with devotion and patience, ensuring that every jar of Gaumaatri ghee carries the essence of authenticity and the blessings of our sacred tradition.

This is not just ghee — it's a gift of wellness from our family to yours.
