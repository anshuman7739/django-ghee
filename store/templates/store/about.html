Pure Desi Ghee's journey began in 2019, fueled by an unwavering passion for purity and a love for organic, wholesome living. Our founder and CEO envisioned reconnecting people with the timeless wisdom of India's Vedic traditions. With years of expertise and a dedication to creating meaningful change, he set out to redefine the way we view food and wellness.
