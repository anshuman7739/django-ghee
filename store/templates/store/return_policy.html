Return & Refund Policy

Gaumaatri - Your Satisfaction is Our Priority

1. Return Eligibility

We accept returns under the following conditions:

  • Time Frame: Within 7 days of delivery
  • Product Condition: Unopened, unused, and in original packaging
  • Seal Intact: Product seal must be unbroken
  • Original Receipt: Order invoice or receipt must be provided
Food Safety Note: Due to the nature of food products, we cannot accept returns of opened or used items unless there is a quality issue.

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened or partially used products
  • Products with broken or tampered seals
  • Items damaged due to misuse or negligence
  • Products purchased during special promotional sales (unless defective)
  • Products returned after 7 days of delivery

3. Valid Reasons for Return

We accept returns for:

  • Wrong Product: Received a different product than ordered
  • Damaged in Transit: Product received in damaged condition
  • Quality Issues: Product not meeting quality standards
  • Expired Product: Product received past expiry date (highly unlikely but covered)
  • Manufacturing Defect: Seal or container defects

4. Return Process

  1. Contact Us
    Reach out within 7 days via email, phone, or WhatsApp with your order number and reason for return.
  2. Approval
    Our team will review your request and approve if eligible. You'll receive return instructions.
  3. Packaging
    Pack the product securely in its original packaging with all accessories and invoice.
  4. Pickup/Shipping
    We'll arrange pickup or provide return shipping address. Keep tracking details safe.
  5. Inspection
    Once received, we'll inspect the product within 2-3 business days.
  6. Refund/Replacement
    After approval, refund or replacement will be processed within 7-10 business days.

5. Refund Methods

  • Original Payment Method: Refunds processed to original payment source
  • UPI Payments: Refunded to the same UPI ID
  • Cash on Delivery: Refunded via bank transfer (account details required)
  • Processing Time: 7-10 business days after approval

Note: Bank processing times may vary. Please allow 5-7 additional days for amount to reflect in your account.

6. Replacement Policy

You may choose replacement instead of refund for:

  • Wrong product delivered
  • Damaged products
  • Quality issues

Replacement products will be shipped within 3-5 business days of approval, subject to stock availability.

7. Return Shipping Costs

  • Our Fault: We cover return shipping for wrong/damaged products
  • Customer Change of Mind: Customer bears return shipping costs (only if unopened)
  • Pickup Service: Free pickup arranged for eligible returns

8. Cancellation Policy

  • Before Dispatch: Orders can be cancelled free of charge
  • After Dispatch: Regular return policy applies
  • How to Cancel: Contact customer service immediately with order number
  • Refund: Full refund processed within 3-5 business days for cancelled orders
Quick Tip: To cancel your order, contact us immediately at +91 7739305520 or via WhatsApp.

9. Quality Guarantee

At Gaumaatri, we stand behind the quality of our products:

  • 100% authentic A2 Desi Ghee made using traditional Bilona method
  • Sourced from healthy, indigenous cows
  • No additives, preservatives, or adulterants
  • Quality tested at every stage

If you're not satisfied with the quality, we'll make it right!

10. Important Notes

  • Always inspect products upon delivery in presence of delivery personnel
  • Report damage or quality issues within 24 hours with photos
  • Keep original packaging and invoice for returns
  • Refund amount does not include shipping charges (except in case of our error)
  • Promotional discounts cannot be refunded separately
