Our Traditional Process

1

Milk Collection

We source milk only from indigenous A2 cows that are naturally grazed and ethically treated.

2

Curd Formation

The milk is allowed to naturally ferment into curd without any additives or preservatives.

3

Butter Extraction

Using the traditional bilona method, butter is hand-churned from the curd.

4

Clarification

The butter is slowly simmered in copper vessels over low heat until all moisture evaporates, leaving behind pure ghee.
